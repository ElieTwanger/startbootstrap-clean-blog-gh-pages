Roadmap Timeline

Each milestone includes a photo, what happened, and what it taught me.

2004 — Redding, California

Born

I was born in Redding, CA in 2004. This is where my story begins.

2004–2023 — Weaverville, California

Raised in Weaverville

I was raised in Weaverville, CA and spent my childhood and teen years building the foundation of who I am through school, community, and work.

June 2023 — Trinity High School

Graduated High School

I graduated from Trinity High School in June 2023. This was a major checkpoint that started my transition into the professional world.

Summer 2023 — U.S. Forest Service

Wildland Firefighter

I worked as a wildland firefighter for the U.S. Forest Service during the summer. It taught me discipline, teamwork under pressure, and how to stay calm in intense situations.

August 2023–May 2024 — Butte College

Started College at Butte

I attended Butte Community College from August 2023 to May 2024. This year helped me strengthen my academic resume and prepare me to transfer to BYU.

January–May 2024 — Butte College

Statistics Tutor

During the school year, I worked as a statistics tutor from January 2024 to May 2024. I learned how to explain topics clearly and help people build confidence and understand complicated statistical concepts in a way that worked for them.

June–August 2024 — LDS Church

Missionary Service

From June 2024 to August 2024, I served as a missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It strengthened my ability to truly love all and empathize with all individuals.

October–December 2024 — Paraeducator

Paraeducator

I worked as a paraeducator from October 2024 to December 2024. This experience improved my patience and responsibility.

January 2025–Present — Brigham Young University

Attending BYU

In January 2025, I started attending BYU, and I still do. This is my current “build” stage where I’m developing skills and preparing for long-term career goals.
